placekitten

A quick and simple service for getting pictures of kittens for
use as placeholders in your designs or code.
Just put your image size (width & height) after our URL and you'll
get a placeholder.

Like this: http://placekitten.com/200/300
or: http://placekitten.com/g/200/300
